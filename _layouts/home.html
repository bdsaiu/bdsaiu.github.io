ABOUT BDSA

Building Community. Celebrating Culture. Creating Impact.

The Bangladeshi Student Association (BDSA) at Indiana University Bloomington is a student-led organization dedicated to connecting Bangladeshi students through cultural events, professional development, community service, and lifelong friendships.
